Iconic Attractions

1. St. Mark's Basilica (Basilica di San Marco)

Description: A masterpiece of Byzantine architecture, St. Mark's Basilica is Venice's most famous church, adorned with stunning mosaics and housing the relics of St. Mark.

Must-See: The Pala d'Oro, an intricate gold altarpiece, and the panoramic view from the basilica's terrace.

2. The Grand Canal (Canal Grande)

Description: The Grand Canal is the main waterway of Venice, lined with magnificent palaces and bustling with gondolas, vaporettos, and water taxis.
